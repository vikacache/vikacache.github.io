Reporting a problem?

Including these in your first email lets us fix things much faster:

  • Unity version - e.g. 2022.3.45f1 (Help → About Unity).
  • Asset version - shown in the Package Manager or the asset's folder.
  • What happened - what you did, what you expected, and what you got instead.
  • Console output - any errors or warnings from the Unity Console, as text or a screenshot.
  • Texture / material details - for MatScale issues: source resolution, format, and the maps involved.

Invoice or refund questions are handled per the Unity Asset Store EULA - include your invoice number from the Asset Store order confirmation.
